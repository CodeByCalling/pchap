## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) – PCHAP Version 3.2**

1. **Is PCHAP an insurance product?**  
   No. PCHAP is not an insurance program and is not regulated by the Insurance Commission. It does not fall under Republic Act 10607 or 9829\.

2. **Are benefits guaranteed?**  
   No. All assistance is subject to fund availability and approval by the Board of Trustees.

3. **What is PCHAP?**  
   PCHAP stands for Pastor’s Care Health Assistance Program, a voluntary mutual aid program for JRM full-time/part time  workers and pastors.

4. **Who can join PCHAP?**  
   Eligible members include JRM pastors, FTWs, pastoral staff, DG heads, and retired workers in good standing.

5. **What is the age requirement for joining?**  
   Applicants must be between 18 and 70 years old at the time of enrollment.

6. **What is the monthly contribution?**  
   Regular members contribute ₱500 per month.

7. **When does membership become effective?**  
   After submission of all required forms and payment of the first contribution.

8. **When am I eligible to request assistance?**  
   After completing six (6) consecutive monthly contributions.

9. **How is the amount of assistance determined?**  
   Based on membership size and fund availability; refer to the projected benefit table.

10. **Is assistance guaranteed even after contributing for 6 months?**  
    After six months of contributions, members may request assistance—but all requests still depend on available funds and board approval.

11. **What happens if I stop contributing?**  
    Missing three (3) consecutive months may lead to suspension of eligibility.

12. **Can I rejoin after being suspended?**  
    Yes, upon updating your contributions and Board approval.

13. **Are contributions refundable?**  
    No. All contributions are non-refundable and non-transferable.

14. **Can spouses and children join?**  
    Yes. Spouses must apply separately. Children under 18 may be listed as dependents.

15. **What if my child is 18 or older?**  
    They must apply independently if they qualify (i.e., FTW, pastor, etc.).

16. **Can retired JRM pastors over 70 join?**  
    Yes, under a special category with a ₱250 monthly contribution.

17. **Will retirees receive the same benefits?**  
    Yes, retirees receive the same benefits as other members, following the same program guidelines and support limitations.

18. **Are health conditions considered?**  
    Members must declare all pre-existing conditions truthfully; these do not disqualify them but may be reviewed.

19. **Is there a required health questionnaire?**  
    Yes. All applicants must complete a health disclosure form.

20. **Can members apply for assistance multiple times?**  
    Members may request assistance once per calendar year, following the program’s regular process and guidelines.

21. **What is the one-time benefit upon withdrawal?**  
    Members who contributed for 5+ years may receive up to ₱10,000 as a one-time grant if they withdraw from the program.

22. **Who operates PCHAP legally?**  
    J29 Foundation, Inc., a separate non-stock, non-profit entity, operates PCHAP.

23. **How is JRM connected to PCHAP?**  
    PCHAP is a ministry project aligned with JRM's mission, and JRM receives annual reports and maintains oversight through a Memorandum of Agreement.

24. **Where can I get more information?**  
    Contact your regional coordinator or any JRM BOT member for more details and a copy of the full policy.
